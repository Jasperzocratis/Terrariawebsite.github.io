About Terarria
Gameplay

Terraria is a 2D sandbox game with gameplay that revolves around exploration, building, crafting, combat, survival, and mining, playable in both single-player and multiplayer modes. The game has a 2D sprite tile-based graphical style reminiscent of the 16-bit sprites found on the Super NES.

The game starts in a procedurally generated world, with players starting out with basic tools and a non-player character (NPC) guide to get them started and bring their attention to aspects of the game and progression. The game's world is made up of several layers of tiles that players can interact with and modify. Many resources, such as metal ores, can be found while exploring caves.

Highlight Bosses

Bosses are aggressive, resilient enemies that offer a substantial challenge to players. Defeating a boss is usually instrumental in advancing the game in some way or another, for example, granting the player a new material for a pickaxe of a higher tier. Each has its own particular way of being summoned.

Moon Lord

The Moon Lord is the final boss in Terraria. Because of its high health, it is generally considered one of the most difficult bosses in the game.

Duke Fishron

Duke Fishron is a tough Hardmode Boss and one of the final Bosses, it can be considered one of the most difficult bosses in the game.

Empress of Light

The Empress of Light is a Hardmode, post-Plantera boss fought in The Hallow. While she can be fought as soon as Plantera is defeated.

Developers

Terraria is an action-adventure sandbox game developed by Re-Logic. The game was first released for Windows on May 16, 2011, and has since been ported to several other platforms.

Re-Logic

Re-Logic is an American independent game developer and publisher based in Indiana. It was founded by Andrew Spinks in 2011.

505 Games

505 Games S.p.A. is an Italian video game publisher based in Milan. It was founded in 2006 as a subsidiary of Milan-based Digital Bros.

Whitney Spinks

Whitney Spinks. Producer: Terraria. Whitney Spinks is known for Terraria (2011), Flipping the Block (2014) and Florida Flippers (2015), Völkers Real Estate Advisor.
